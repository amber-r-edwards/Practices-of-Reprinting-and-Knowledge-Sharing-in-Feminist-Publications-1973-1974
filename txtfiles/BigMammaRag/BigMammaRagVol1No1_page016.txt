SYLVIA PLATH died on a Monday morning
around 6:00 A.M. She went downstairs
into the kitchen and left

a plate of bread and butter and a

Then she opened the oven door,

Her book "The Bell Jar" is widely acclaimed now, and her two books of
poetry, "Colossus" and "Ariel" are
a common sight in all bookstores. 

Still - I wonder how many of her
readers have the power over her life that
will allow her to end the nightmare
that an analytical perceptive
realistic mind shows her daily.

She decides to commit suicide.
Suicide, the last attempt at self
determination, real control over one's
life, a desperate and liberating act.
