UNION WOMEN

The Coalition of Labor Union Women (CLUW) was
formed in Chicago in March 1974. CLUW's pur-
poses are to pursue political and legislative
action in the workplace and stronger representation
of women in union leadership. A CLUW organ-
izational meeting will be held June 6 at

7:30 p.m. at the United Electrical Hall,

3039 Central Ave. N.E. in Minneapolis. This
will be an opportunity for Twin Cities women
to learn about the Chicago convention and to
discuss issues concerning union women. All
bona fide union women are urged to attend.
