Alternative Readers Service wishes to announce that the first issue of  
the Froggie Hollow Gazette, a six-hour tape, is now available for free, to the  
blind, visually handicapped, and functionally illiterate.  

The first issue contains readings from the Liberation News Service, The  
Whe Eargh Catalogue, The Mother Earth News, The Rolling Stone, and several other radical or underground sources.  

Our purpose is two-fold. We are available to read anything the blind or illiterate are hassled with getting through conventional channels. We also hope to become a focus for an embryonic handicapped person's liberation movement.  

We need lots of publicity. The group of people we hope to reach is not presently part of any organization, so we must depend on readers of the underground press to turn their blind friends on to our service. We would appreciate a small continuing spot in your paper. We would be ecstatic with a free subscription and could perhaps work out an exchange.
